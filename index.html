About Aarambh

Aarambh is the annual flagship cultural fest that brings together creativity, energy, and talent under one roof. From electrifying performances to immersive experiences, it is a celebration of youth and passion. Aarambh 6.0 promises to be bigger, bolder, and more unforgettable than ever before.

Recruitment Roles

Event Team

Plan, coordinate, and oversee every aspect of Aarambh 6.0. The Event Team ensures smooth execution by connecting all departments and managing the overall flow of the fest.

Management Team

Manage the core operations of the event including logistics, coordination, discipline, technical support, stage management, and ground execution to ensure everything runs efficiently.

Cultural Team

Organize and coordinate all cultural activities including dance, music, drama, fashion, and other performances while ensuring participants have a seamless experience.

Dance Team

Handle dance auditions, rehearsals, choreography coordination, backstage support, and competition management throughout the event.

Singing Team

Coordinate singing auditions, rehearsals, sound checks, artist requirements, and singing competitions from start to finish.

Drama Team

Manage theatre performances, skits, scripts, rehearsals, props, and stage coordination for all dramatic events.

Discipline Team

Maintain discipline, crowd control, volunteer coordination, security support, and ensure a safe and organized environment during the fest.

Technical Team

Handle sound systems, lighting, LED screens, projectors, photography, videography, livestreams, and all technical requirements of Aarambh

Social Media & PR Team

Promote Aarambh through social media campaigns, content creation, photography, public relations, influencer outreach, and digital marketing.

Decoration Team

Design and decorate the venue, stage, entrances, photo booths, and event spaces to create an attractive and memorable festival atmosphere.

Design Team

Craft stunning visuals, posters, and digital assets. Bring the creative identity of Aarambh to life through design.

Sponsorship Team

Secure partnerships and funding. Pitch to brands and manage sponsor relations to make Aarambh a grand success.

Anchoring Team

Be the voice of Aarambh. Host events, engage the crowd, and keep the energy alive on stage.

Hospitality Team

Welcome guests, judges, celebrities, sponsors, and participants. Manage registrations, refreshments, and ensure every guest has a comfortable experience.
